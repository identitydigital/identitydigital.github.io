About Us

We are Identity Digital, a tight-knit group of friends from the UK with a shared passion for coding, creativity, and innovation. What started as casual brainstorming sessions over coffee (and way too many late nights) has grown into a series of exciting projects that we’re thrilled to share with the world.

Our team thrives on turning unique ideas into reality, and Oracle AI is one of those creations born out of curiosity and our love for building fun, useful tools. We believe in crafting technology that’s simple, efficient, and accessible to everyone.

When we’re not coding, you’ll probably find us discussing new tech trends, gaming together, or hunting for inspiration for our next big idea. Thanks for checking out our work—it means the world to us!

- The Identity Digital Team
